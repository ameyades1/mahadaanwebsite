Introduction

A Land Offering That Transcends Time and Generations

"भूमिदानं महादानं सर्वपुण्यफलं भवेत्।"

(Sanatan Scriptures)

"Bhoomi Daan is the highest offering, yielding immense merit and blessings."

The Garud Puran (Ch. 8) states:

"By donating land to a Saint, Brahmin, or a Jeevit Sadguru, a man is freed from all sins, including the gravest of karmas."

Bhoomi Daan is not just a donation—it is the foundation for Dharma, knowledge, and spiritual upliftment. By offering land or contributing towards procuring it, you preserve Sanatan wisdom, uplift communities, and create centres that serve seekers for generations.

Your land donation becomes a sanctuary of knowledge, healing, and self-realisation.

02

Why Donate for Bhoomi Daan?

Be a Part of a Divine Mission to Transform Bharat into a Vishwa Guru

For centuries, Bhoomi Daan has been regarded as the most meritorious donation, supporting temples, ashrams, gurukuls, and spiritual centres. Kings, sages, and devotees have offered land to ensure the preservation of Dharma and Vedic wisdom.

Why Now?

In an era where spirituality is diminishing and land is commercialised, your Bhoomi Daan will:

Build Antar Yog Spiritual & Educational Centres – Places of learning, meditation, and healing that will transform thousands of lives.
Preserve and Spread Ancient Wisdom – Reviving sacred teachings backed by scientific reasoning.
Support Those in Need – Providing a sanctuary for seekers, students, and sadhaks to immerse in sadhana.
Revive Bharat’s Rich Heritage – Strengthening Dharma, ensuring Bharat regains its rightful place as Vishwa Guru.
This is more than a donation—it is a spiritual investment in the future of Dharma.
