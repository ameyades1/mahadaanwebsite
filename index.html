Mahadaan Initiative

Sacred Giving Platform

A complete wireframe for the Antaryog Foundation's Mahadaan (sacred giving) campaign. The site features 9 responsive sections showcasing donation pathways, spiritual impact, and transformation stories.

Built with the v7 theme palette — copper and gold accents on warm cream backgrounds. Fully responsive from mobile (375px) to desktop (1920px) with smooth scroll interactions and animated statistics.
