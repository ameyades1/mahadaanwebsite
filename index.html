About Mahadaan

Mahadaan—meaning "the great gift" in Sanskrit—is Antaryog Foundation's transformative initiative to support spiritual seekers and wellness practitioners on their journey of growth and self-discovery.

Every contribution, regardless of size, fuels programs that bring ancient yogic wisdom into modern practice, creating lasting impact across our global community.

Your donation directly supports:

  • Yoga and meditation teacher training
  • Wellness retreats and shibirs
  • Accessible programs for underserved communities
  • Research in traditional healing practices

Our Mission

To serve as a bridge between ancient spiritual traditions and contemporary wellness needs, empowering individuals and communities to achieve balance, health, and transformation through authentic yogic practices.

Programs Supported by Mahadaan

Comprehensive 200-hour and advanced certifications for aspiring yoga instructors and wellness facilitators.

Immersive shibirs and retreats combining yoga, meditation, pranayama, and holistic wellness practices.
