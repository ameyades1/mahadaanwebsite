True Abundance Lies in Giving, Not Accumulating

Every day, millions of hardworking individuals fight hunger, illness, and despair—despite their dedication and effort.

A child sleeping hungry, dreaming of just one warm meal.

02

Why Donate for the Poor?

Your Seva is a Direct Offering to the Divine

📖 Scriptural References on Charity (Daan)

Bhagavad Gita (17.20) – "Charity given at the right time, to the right person, without expectation, is sattvic and bestows the highest blessings."
Manusmriti (4.226) – "One who feeds the hungry, clothes the poor, and shelters the homeless accumulates the greatest punya and divine favour."
Garud Puran – "A portion of one’s wealth must always be set aside for feeding the hungry, for such an act dissolves sins and grants prosperity."

How Your Contribution Changes Lives

✅ Provides Food & Shelter – Nourishing underprivileged families and individuals struggling to survive.
✅ Offers Basic Necessities – Supplying clothing, hygiene products, and daily essentials.
✅ Supports Education for Poor Children – Funding books, school fees, and skill-building programs.
✅ Provides Medical Aid & Healthcare – Ensuring lifesaving treatments and wellness support.
"Every grain of food given with love becomes a seed of prosperity in your own life."

Who Benefits from Your Daan?

A Helping Hand Reaches Where It’s Needed Most

✔ Families and daily wage workers who work hard yet barely survive.
✔ Underprivileged children and orphans needing education and nourishment.
✔ Elderly and sick individuals unable to access basic healthcare and support.
✔ The homeless and destitute, ensuring they receive food, warmth, and dignity.
